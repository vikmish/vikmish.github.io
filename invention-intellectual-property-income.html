WEEK XIV

Invention, Intellectual Property & Income

This week, active discussions on the values and types of Intellectual properties took place. To generate realistic scenarios and proposals, Smart Citizen Kit was explored as the focus. The aim was to understand the data, variables and intellectual  properties involved to strategise ways to better the system in next five years. The team I was involved with focused on the trajectory of the SCK if it followed the adherence towards the same systemic values as currently. To make the solutions and system more distributed, we suggested certain extensions and advancements to sustain the product alongside the relevant system and market globally.
